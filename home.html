Welcome to Diabetes Prediction System

Type1:

diabetes is believed to be an autoimmune condition.This means your immune system mistakenly attacks and destroys the beta cells in your pancreas that produce insulin. The damage is permanent

Type 2:

diabetes starts as insulin resistance.This means your body can’t use insulin efficiently. That stimulates your pancreas to produce more insulin until it keep up demand. which leads to high blood sugar

Gestational diabetes:

Gestational diabetesis due to insulin-blocking hormones produced during pregnancy. women who don’t already have diabetes. Every year 2% to 10% of pregnancies are affected by gestational diabetes.
